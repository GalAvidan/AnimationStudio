# Explainer Starter Script

## Working Title

Why animation helps explanations stick

## Script

Sometimes an idea is hard to understand because every part arrives at once.

Animation lets us slow the idea down.

First, we show the problem.

Then we reveal the structure.

Finally, we connect the pieces so the viewer can remember the point.

The goal is not more decoration. The goal is a clearer explanation.

## Notes

- Target runtime: 10 seconds.
- Tone: calm, direct, useful.
- Visual direction: simple shapes, clear motion, high readability.
